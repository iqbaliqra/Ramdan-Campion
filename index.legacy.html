🤲 Essential Ramadan Duas
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
Allahumma innaka 'afuwwun, tuhibbul-'afwa, fa'fu 'anni
"O Allah, You are forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me."
Laylatul Qadr
اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ آمَنْتُ وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ أَفْطَرْتُ
Allahumma laka sumtu wa bika aamantu wa 'ala rizqika aftartu
"O Allah, I fasted for You, I believe in You, and I break my fast with Your sustenance."
Breaking Fast (Iftar)
ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ وَابْتَلَّتِ الْعُرُوقُ وَثَبَتَ الْأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ
Dhahab az-zama', wabtallatil-'urooq, wa thabatal-ajru inshaa-Allah
"The thirst is gone, the veins are moistened and the reward is confirmed, if Allah wills."
After Iftar
وَبِصَوْمِ غَدٍ نَوَيْتُ مِنْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ
Wa bisawmi ghadin nawaitu min shahri Ramadan
"I intend to keep the fast for tomorrow in the month of Ramadan."
Suhoor Intention (Niyyah)
اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي وَاهْدِنِي وَارْزُقْنِي
Allahummaghfirli warhamni wahdini warzuqni
"O Allah, forgive me, have mercy on me, guide me, and provide for me."
General Daily Dua
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْكَسَلِ وَالْهَرَمِ
Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-kasali wal-haram
"O Allah, I seek refuge in You from laziness and old age."
